SECTION 1 — THE PROBLEM
Headline

Europe Builds on Infrastructure It Doesn’t Control

Modern applications depend on backend platforms for authentication, databases, storage, and serverless logic. Today, most of these platforms are operated by non-European companies and subject to foreign jurisdiction — even when data is stored in Europe.

For startups, this may seem invisible.
For regulated industries, it is a growing risk.

Developers face a frustrating trade-off:

Choose world-class developer experience, or

Choose legal certainty and data sovereignty.

Europe deserves both.
